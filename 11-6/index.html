How to make a sandwich:


  1. Get bread
  2. Get deli meat
  3. Get condiments
  4. Get utensils
  5. Get plate
  6. Cut bread and get 2 slices of bread
  7. Put condiments on first slice of bread
  8. Put deli meat on next
  9. Put the second slice of bread on top
  10. Done
