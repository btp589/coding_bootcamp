position: static (Default)
- Render on page according to natural flow of HTML

position: relative
- Nudge the boxes in relation to their "original" location.

position: absolute
- Uses coordinates (similar to (x,y) coordinates)
- Will not scroll with page.

position: fixed
- Uses coordinates but stays even while the page is scrolled, such as navigation bars.


Layering with Z-Index
- Allows for layered elements on top of each other when they're positioned.
