Grid Layout
-----------
Rows are containers
Columns are where contents reside

Bootstrap Grid
--------------
Bootstrap lets you organize content based on the "number of columns" a div spans - out of a total 12 columns.
Ex:
<div class="col-md-12">
(spans full width)

<div class="col-md-4>
(spans 1/3 of full width)
