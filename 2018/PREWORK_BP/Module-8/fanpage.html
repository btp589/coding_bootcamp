History

Founded in February 1991 under the name Silicon & Synapse by Michael Morhaime, Frank Pearce and Allen Adham. The company then later renamed to Blizzard Entertainment in 1994. Eventually, Blizzard Entertainment merged with Activision in 1998 and became an independent company in 2013.

One of Blizzard's most popular game of all time is World of Warcraft. If you have ever played a PC game before, Chances are very good that you have heard of World of Warcraft (or WOW). At its peak, WOW had 12 million subscribers. Even though the game was created in 2004, 14 years ago, there are still hundreds of thousands of players still playing it today.
